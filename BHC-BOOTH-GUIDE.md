## 📱 Demo Flow (Show on Tablet)

1. **Launch the course** — Click the link, shows instant access
2. **Select a surgery type** — Show personalization (e.g., "Knee Replacement")
3. **Flip through 2-3 modules** — Show evidence-based content + interactive elements
4. **Show progress tracking** — "See? Their progress is saved automatically"
5. **Mention the certificate** — "When they complete all modules + quizzes, they get a personalized certificate"
6. **Close with:** "Want to share this with your patients? Use this QR code or the link on the card."

**If Slow:** Demo on tablet to attract attention  
**If Busy:** Hand out cards + QR code, capture email, follow up later

- *"Is this just an online course?"* → "It's interactive with personalized modules, progress tracking, and a real certificate. But yes, it's digital — that means patients can study anytime, anywhere."
