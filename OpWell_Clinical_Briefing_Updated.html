This clinical briefing evaluates the foundational peer-reviewed literature supporting the OpWell Concierge™ care delivery model. Designed as an independent, telehealth-based perioperative specialty resource for healthcare providers and surgical teams, OpWell translates multicenter randomized controlled trials (RCTs), Enhanced Recovery After Surgery (ERAS) pathways, and Perioperative Surgical Home (PSH) data into a turnkey referral option that optimizes clinical outcomes and safeguards procedural revenue.

Empirical Framework & Peer-Reviewed Evidence

The individual medical optimization, structured prehabilitation, and continuous 30-day symptom tracking protocols provided by OpWell Concierge correlate directly with statistically significant reductions in hospital lengths of stay, avoidable emergency room visits, and costly day-of-surgery cancellations.

B2B Integration & Clinical Continuity

OpWell Concierge operates entirely as a collaborative, premium extension of your existing clinical practice. We do not alter surgical plans, manage technical operative complications, or replace standard post-operative office visits. By intercepting low-risk programmatic queries, providing objective medical clearance reporting, and delivering a validated Readiness Score alongside a physical completion certificate, OpWell ensures your patients arrive optimized, compliant, and structurally prepared for the stress of anesthesia and surgical intervention.
