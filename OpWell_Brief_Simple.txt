EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

OpWell Concierge™ is an independent, telehealth-based perioperative specialty service for healthcare providers and surgical teams. It translates multicenter RCT evidence from Enhanced Recovery After Surgery (ERAS), Perioperative Surgical Home (PSH), and prehabilitation literature into a turnkey referral model that optimizes surgical patients across three phases: preoperative preparation, intraoperative coordination, and structured 30-day post-discharge monitoring. 

---

KEY EVIDENCE CLAIMS (with Effect Estimates & Certainty)

1. COMPLICATION REDUCTION VIA EXERCISE PREHABILITATION
   Effect: OR 0.50 (0.39–0.64); ~50% relative reduction
   Certainty: Very Low (robust after exclusion of high-ROB trials)
   Source: McIsaac et al., BMJ 2025; 186 RCTs, n = 15,684

2. COMPLICATION REDUCTION VIA ERAS PATHWAYS
   Effect: RR 0.71 (0.59–0.87)
   Certainty: Moderate-High
   Source: Sauro et al., JAMA Netw Open 2024; 74 RCTs, n = 9,076

3. HOSPITAL LOS REDUCTION VIA ERAS
   Effect: −1.88 days (−2.81 to −0.95)
   Certainty: Moderate
   Source: Sauro et al., JAMA Netw Open 2024

4. COST SAVINGS (Non-colorectal Abdominal ERAS)
   Effect: −$5,109 per case (−$5,852 to −$4,366)
   Certainty: Moderate
   Source: Visioni et al., Ann Surg 2018; 39 studies, n = 6,511

5. ED VISIT REDUCTION VIA MHEALTH MONITORING
   Effect: OR 0.42 (0.23–0.79); ~58% reduction
   Certainty: Low
   Source: Dawes et al., BJS 2021; 45 studies

6. READMISSION REDUCTION VIA MHEALTH MONITORING
   Effect: OR 0.47 (0.29–0.77); ~53% reduction
   Certainty: Low
   Source: Dawes et al., BJS 2021

7. QOR-15 PSYCHOMETRIC VALIDITY
   Effect: Cronbach's α = 0.85 (0.83–0.87); test-retest r = 0.97
   Certainty: High
   Source: Myles et al., Br J Anaesth 2022; 26 studies, 16 countries

CRITICAL DIFFERENTIATOR: STRUCTURED VS. PASSIVE MONITORING

Simple telephone follow-up ≠ structured digital monitoring.

Boggan et al. (2025, Annals of Internal Medicine) — 7 RCTs, n = 7,075:
→ Single-contact telephone calls within 7 days: NO reduction in readmissions or ED visits

OpWell's structured, multi-contact digital platform:
→ OR 0.42–0.67 for readmissions and ED visits

This is THE competitive advantage. Passive follow-up doesn't work. Structured monitoring does.

B2B INTEGRATION & CLINICAL CONTINUITY

OpWell operates as a collaborative extension of your existing practice:
✓ Does NOT alter surgical plans
✓ Does NOT manage technical operative complications
✓ Does NOT replace standard postoperative office visits

By intercepting low-risk programmatic queries, providing objective medical clearance reporting, and delivering a validated Readiness Score, OpWell ensures patients arrive optimized, compliant, and structurally prepared for anesthesia and surgery.

---

TRANSPARENCY ON EVIDENCE CERTAINTY

STRONGEST (Moderate-High): ERAS pathways consistently reduce LOS and complications across high-quality RCTs.

STRONG (Very Low to Moderate): Prehabilitation shows 50–54% complication reduction; results robust after high-ROB exclusion.

MODERATE (Low-Moderate): Post-discharge monitoring works best with structured digital platforms, NOT passive calls.

FOUNDATIONAL (Low): PSH model lacks RCTs; limited to before-and-after cohort data from single centers.

Bottom Line: This brief proactively addresses evidence limitations. Rigor beats oversimplification.
