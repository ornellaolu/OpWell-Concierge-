Why I Built OpWell

Most patients meet their anesthesiologist 5 minutes before surgery. By then, it's too late to optimize risk factors, manage medications, or address anxiety. Complications that could have been prevented are baked in.

I built OpWell to change that. To give patients access to an anesthesiologist weeks before surgery — not minutes. To optimize the parts of perioperative care that actually matter. To reduce anxiety, catch risks early, and set realistic expectations.

Surgery is personal. Your preparation should be, too. That's what OpWell is built around.

My Philosophy

Evidence-based care: Every recommendation is grounded in clinical evidence and guidelines from professional organizations — ERAS, ASA, ASMBS.

Individualized approach: Your comorbidities, medications, anxiety, and surgical goals are unique. Your preparation should reflect that.

Patient partnership: I'm not here to direct your care — I'm here to inform you and support your decisions. Informed patients make better choices.

What Patients Say
