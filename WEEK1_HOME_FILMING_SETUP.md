## HOME FILMING SETUP (5 minutes prep)

### Location
- **Where**: Anywhere in your home with clean background (blank wall, bookshelf, kitchen counter)
- **Avoid**: Messy background, window reflections, busy patterns
- **Best**: Neutral wall + good lighting

### Lighting (Most Important)
- **Best**: Natural light from a window (sit facing the window, let light hit your face)
- **Good**: Overhead ceiling light + phone light/desk lamp bounced off wall
- **Ring light**: If you have one, perfect—use it
- **Do NOT**: Film with window behind you (you'll be backlit/dark)

### Phone Setup
- **Mount**: Phone stand pointing at face, at eye level
- **Distance**: 2-3 feet from phone (medium shot, not too close)
- **Angle**: Slight upward angle (flattering, professional)
- **Test**: Record 10 seconds, check focus and framing

### Audio
- **Use**: Phone's built-in mic (it's fine for this)
- **Location**: Quiet room, no TV, no background noise
- **Test**: Record 10 seconds, listen on earbuds—can you hear yourself clearly?

### Clothing
- **Wear**: Professional but not stiff (button-up shirt, solid color, no logos)
- **Color**: Avoid white or very bright (contrasts with sage green brand color)
- **Avoid**: Busy patterns, stripes, high-necks

## SCRIPT NOTES FOR TOMORROW

### Delivery Tips
1. **Don't memorize word-for-word** — know the points, speak naturally
2. **Pause for emphasis** — after the hook, let it land
3. **Vary tone** — start serious/authoritative, become warm/empowering by the end
4. **Look at camera** — pretend you're talking to one person
5. **Breathe** — if you mess up mid-section, pause, take a breath, start again (edit later)
6. **Multiple takes matter** — do 2-3 of each, pick the most natural

### What "Feels Right"
- Authentic (not corporate)
- Warm (not cold/clinical)
- Clear (no mumbling, pace is easy to follow)
- Confident (you know this material)

### Common Mistakes to Avoid
- ❌ Reading directly from phone (looks awkward)
- ❌ Speaking too fast (slow it down slightly)
- ❌ Stiff posture (relax shoulders, be human)
- ❌ Too much hand movement (keep hands relaxed or use for emphasis on key points)
- ❌ Fake smile at the end (authentic > perfect)
