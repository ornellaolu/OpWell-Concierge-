    Clinical Disclaimer

    This tool is a clinical decision support instrument designed to assist physician assessment — it does not replace clinical judgment. All scores, risk categories, and recommendations should be interpreted in the context of the patient's full clinical picture. Emergency red flags always warrant immediate evaluation regardless of composite score.

    POD-adjusted scoring provides contextual modifiers (e.g., higher pain and reduced mobility are expected in early recovery). These modifiers do not override clinical concern — if something doesn't look right, escalate.
