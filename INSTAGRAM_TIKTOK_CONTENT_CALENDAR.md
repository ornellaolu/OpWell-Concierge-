**Format for CapCut**:

### WEEK 7: Informed Consent + Patient Education
**Title**: "Questions to Ask Your Surgeon (That Actually Matter)"
**Pillar**: Patient Education + Safety & Expertise
**Theme**: Informed Consent
**Format**:
- IG: Carousel (6-7 slides, one question per slide) or Reel (90 sec)
- TikTok: Series of 2-3 quick clips (15-20 sec each) = 45-60 sec total

**Hook**: "Here are the 7 questions most patients never ask (but should)"

**Key Points** (one per slide/clip):
1. "What specifically are you operating on?" (patients often don't know exact anatomy)
2. "What's your complication rate for THIS procedure?" (not general rates)
3. "What happens if you discover something unexpected mid-op?"
4. "What should I do if I'm not recovering normally?"
5. "When can I return to normal activities?" (be specific: driving, work, exercise)
6. "What's your communication plan if something goes wrong?"
7. "Can I speak with someone pre-op to ask questions?"

**Why This Works**:
- Directly ties to informed consent theme
- Actionable (patients can use immediately)
- Empowers patients in surgeon conversations
- High-engagement carousel format

**CTA**: "Write these down before your surgeon consultation"

### WEEK 8: Labor & Delivery + Myth-Busting
**Title**: "Epidural Myths Busted (By an Anesthesiologist)"
**Pillar**: Myth-Busting + Safety & Expertise
**Theme**: Labor & Delivery
**Format**:
- IG: Reel (90 sec) with text overlays
- TikTok: 45-60 sec video

**Hook**: "Every pregnant patient asks me the same 5 questions about epidurals. Let's bust these myths."

**Myths to Address**:
1. **Myth**: "Epidural will paralyze me" → **Truth**: You can move your legs, feel contractions, push effectively
2. **Myth**: "Epidural causes back pain" → **Truth**: No causal link; pregnancy/labor cause back pain
3. **Myth**: "I'll have a headache for months" → **Truth**: Rare (1 in 500), usually resolves in days
4. **Myth**: "It won't work if I need it" → **Truth**: Works 85-90% of the time; alternatives exist
5. **Myth**: "Epidural slows labor" → **Truth**: May lengthen labor by 1-2 hours, but safer recovery outcomes

**Why This Works**:
- High-anxiety audience (pregnant patients researching obsessively)
- Myth-busting = highly shareable
- Positions you as trustworthy source
- Surgeon partners appreciate evidence-based communication

**CTA**: "Expecting? Let's talk through your pain management options"

## PRODUCTION NOTES FOR CAPCUT

### Video Templates (Reusable Across Posts)
1. **Direct-to-Camera** (60-90 sec)
   - You speaking naturally in clinic/workspace
   - B-roll of equipment, workspace (HIPAA-safe)
   - Text overlays with key points
   - Fade to OpWell logo + CTA

2. **Carousel Adaptation** (Instagram)
   - 5-7 slides, one idea per slide
   - First slide: Hook/question
   - Slides 2-6: Answer (text + small imagery/icon)
   - Last slide: CTA + contact info

3. **Text-Heavy Myth-Busting** (CapCut favorite)
   - "MYTH:" appears on screen
   - You respond (3-5 sec clip)
   - "TRUTH:" overlay with answer
   - Repeat for 2-3 myths
   - Upbeat music/transitions (CapCut templates)

### Audio/Music Recommendations
- Keep background simple (slight ambient, not distracting)
- For myth-busting: Upbeat, energetic music
- For serious topics (informed consent): Calm, professional tone
- CapCut has free trending sounds—use sparingly, keep medical credibility

## POSTING STRATEGY

### Instagram (1 post/week)
- **Best days**: Tuesday-Thursday (9am or 6pm EST)
- **Format rotation**: 
  - Week 1: Reel + carousel post (as follow-up)
  - Week 2: Reel + single image
  - Week 3: Carousel
  - Etc. (vary to test what resonates)
- **Captions**: 200-300 words, include CTA, use relevant hashtags
- **Hashtags** (research these): #PreOp #AnesthetiologyLife #PatientEducation #SurgicalPrep #MedicalTourism #LaborAndDelivery #InformedConsent #OBGYNPartner

### TikTok (1 post/week)
- **Post frequency**: 3-4pm or 7-9pm EST (when your target demographic scrolls)
- **Format**: 30-90 seconds, vertical, highly engaging hook in first 3 seconds
- **Captions**: Shorter than Instagram, trending sounds optional
- **Hashtags**: #MedicalTok #PreOp #AnesthetiologyTok #PatientEducation #PregnantAndPrepared

### Cross-Posting Strategy
- Post IG Reel first, wait 1-2 days, repurpose for TikTok
- Tailor captions to each platform (IG longer, TikTok snappier)
- Don't crosspost simultaneously (TikTok algorithm penalizes duplicates)
