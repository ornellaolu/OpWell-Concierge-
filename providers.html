Why Partner With OpWell

The Core Value Props

Four clinical and operational imperatives that drive measurable outcomes in surgical partnerships.

Protect Your OR Timeline & Reduce Cancellations

Last-minute surgical cancellations are a major source of lost revenue and scheduling disruption. Preoperative anesthesia consultations actively identify and stabilize underlying physiological stressors weeks in advance. By executing a strict preoperative medication audit and implementing our Clinical Supplement & Tissue Recovery Protocol, we eliminate the primary drivers of day-of-surgery cancellations due to unchecked medical risks or supplement interference.

Offload the Administrative and Coordination Burden

The OpWell concierge model shifts the weight of medical optimization completely away from your practice staff. Instead of your office chasing down medical clearances, managing medication adjustments, or organizing specialist referrals, Dr. Oluwole handles this comprehensively—from the initial surgical decision through 30 days post-discharge. This frees you to focus entirely on operative planning and technical execution.

Minimize Readmissions & Secure Post-Op Recovery

The first 30 days post-discharge represent a massive vulnerability gap, especially for medically complex patients or those living alone. OpWell provides a proactive safety net during this critical window with structured recovery tracking, medication compliance tracking, and asynchronous physician messaging. We catch and triage normal recovery hurdles early, drastically reducing failure-to-rescue events and preventing unnecessary ER visits.

Drive Quantifiable Financial & Value-Based Outcomes

In an era of bundled payments and value-based metrics, clinical partnerships with an anesthesiologist-led perioperative surgical home model deliver measurable economic advantages. Peer-reviewed health economic analyses demonstrate substantial cost savings and exceptional quality metric performance across surgical specialties.
