What Our Pre Op Consultation Includes

OpWell's pre op consultation and surgery preparation program ensures you arrive at your procedure optimized and genuinely ready — whether you're having cosmetic surgery, bariatric surgery, or any elective procedure.

  • 50-minute pre-op consultation with a board-certified anesthesiologist
  • Comprehensive anesthesia & aspiration risk assessment
  • Personalized prehabilitation protocol
  • Medication & supplement safety review
  • Mental wellness specialist access
  • Realistic recovery timeline & expectation-setting
  • VTE blood clot risk assessment

Specialized by Procedure Type

Cosmetic Surgery

BBL, rhinoplasty, breast augmentation, tummy tuck, liposuction, and more. Optimize your pre-op nutrition, manage anxiety, and set realistic recovery expectations.

Bariatric Surgery

Specialized pre-op risk assessment for gastric bypass, sleeve gastrectomy, and revision surgeries. Includes psychological clearance and evidence-based prehabilitation.

General Surgery

Comprehensive perioperative assessment for hernia repair, gallbladder surgery, and other general procedures with anesthesiology expertise.

Why OpWell?

Anesthesiologist-Led Care: Most patients meet their anesthesiologist 5 minutes before surgery. OpWell gives you a board-certified anesthesiologist in your corner weeks before you reach the OR.

Prevent Complications: Many surgical complications — poor wound healing, medication interactions, prolonged recovery, preventable anxiety — are rooted in what happens before surgery. We help you optimize every dimension.

No Referral Required: Book directly. Telehealth consultations available. HSA and FSA accepted.
